# Communications – ICAN

This folder contains all **public-facing communication materials** for the Iowa Cannabis Action Network (ICAN).

## Purpose
To maintain a consistent, compliant, and credible communication voice across:
- Social media
- Press releases
- Public statements
- Educational materials
- Brand identity
- Advocacy updates

## Subfolders
- `press/` – Press releases & media statements  
- `social/` – Social posts, graphics copy, content calendar  
- `brand-guide/` – Logos, color codes, typography, usage rules  
- `messaging/` – Talking points, tone guidelines, approved language  
- `templates/` – Reusable files (press templates, one-pagers, graphics text)

## What Belongs Here
- All public-facing text
- Branding assets
- Communication frameworks
- Press kits & statements
- Social advocacy content (nonpartisan)

## What Does NOT Belong Here
- Internal strategies (should be in `/sops`)
- Political messaging for PAC or 527
- Donor-specific language
