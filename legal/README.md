# Legal – ICAN

This folder contains **governance, compliance, and legal documents** for Iowa Cannabis Action Network, Inc.

## Purpose
To maintain:
- Articles of Incorporation
- Bylaws
- Conflict of Interest Policy
- Board Resolutions
- Organizational Meeting Minutes
- Corporate Binder index
- State and federal filings
- Required compliance records

## Subfolders
- `governance/` – Articles, Bylaws, COI Policy, Binder Index  
- `resolutions/` – EIN, banking, 8976, officer appointments, etc.  
- `filings/` – State filings, IRS receipts, biennial reports  
- `compliance/` – Policies, calendars, checklists, reporting logs  

## Notes
- This is a **C4-only** legal folder.  
- Do not include PAC or 527 filings in this repo.  
- Maintain clear entity separation at all times.
