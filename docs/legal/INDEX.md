# ICAN Legal Document Index

These records collectively form the official legal file for Iowa Cannabis Action Network, Inc. (ICAN), documenting its status as a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization under the Iowa Nonprofit Corporation Act and the Internal Revenue Code.

## Formation Documents
- [Articles of Incorporation](articles-of-incorporation.pdf): The signed and filed Articles that legally create ICAN as an Iowa nonprofit corporation and outline its purpose and registered agent.
- [EIN Confirmation Letter](ein-confirmation-letter.pdf): The IRS letter assigning ICAN Employer Identification Number 41-2746368 for federal tax and banking purposes.

## Governance Framework
- [Bylaws of ICAN](bylaws-of-ican.pdf): Board-approved governance rules describing director duties, officer roles, meetings, and decision-making processes.
- [Conflict of Interest Policy](conflict-of-interest-policy.pdf): Policy required by the IRS that guides directors and officers to disclose and manage potential conflicts.

## Organizational Actions
- [Organizational Meeting Packet](organizational-meeting-packet.pdf): Agenda, minutes, and unanimous written consents establishing the initial board actions and approvals.
- [Board, Officer, Banking, EIN, Fiscal Year, and Form 8976 Resolutions](board-and-officer-resolutions.pdf): Consolidated resolutions authorizing leadership roles, banking authority, fiscal year adoption, and the filing of IRS Form 8976.

## Regulatory Filings and Receipts
- [IRS Form 8976 Payment Confirmation Letter](irs-form-8976-payment-confirmation.pdf): Proof that ICAN submitted and paid for the required IRS notice to operate as a 501(c)(4).
