# ICAN Volunteer Onboarding Guide

## Step 1 — Submit Volunteer Application
Volunteers complete the online form including:
- Contact info  
- Skills & interests  
- Role preferences  
- Consent & agreements  

## Step 2 — Welcome Email
Includes:
- Handbook  
- Code of Conduct  
- Program overview  
- Slack/Discord or email instructions  

## Step 3 — Orientation
Volunteers attend:
- 15–20 minute intro  
- Overview of ICAN mission  
- Explanation of C4 rules  
- Introduction to Victory Gardens  

## Step 4 — Role Assignment
Based on:
- Interest  
- Skills  
- Availability  
- Region  

## Step 5 — Training
Volunteers receive role-specific materials (see training curriculum).

## Step 6 — First Assignment
Volunteers may be asked to:
- Assist at an event  
- Participate in a garden build  
- Help with outreach  
- Create digital content  

## Step 7 — Tracking & Feedback
Volunteers remain in contact with ICAN for:
- Updates  
- Opportunities  
- Feedback  
- Recognition
