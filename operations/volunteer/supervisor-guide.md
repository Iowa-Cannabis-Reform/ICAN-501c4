# ICAN Volunteer Supervisor Guide

Supervisors are responsible for:
- Welcoming volunteers  
- Providing clear instructions  
- Ensuring safety  
- Maintaining compliance  
- Supporting garden partners  

## Expectations
- Be responsive and clear  
- Encourage volunteers  
- Document incidents  
- Uphold ICAN rules  

## Not Allowed
- Giving political tasks  
- Using volunteers for non-ICAN work  
- Sharing internal strategy outside ICAN
