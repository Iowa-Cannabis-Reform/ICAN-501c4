# ICAN Volunteer Code of Conduct

All volunteers must adhere to the following standards:

## 1. Respect
Treat all community members, staff, and volunteers with courtesy and dignity.

## 2. Safety
Follow all safety protocols during events, garden activities, and outreach.

## 3. Integrity
Represent ICAN honestly and fairly.  
Do not misrepresent your role or our policies.

## 4. Nonpartisanship
ICAN is a 501(c)(4).  
Volunteers **may not** endorse candidates or advocate for political campaigns while representing ICAN.

## 5. Confidentiality
Do not share private information without consent.

## 6. Professionalism
Arrive prepared, communicate clearly, and follow event protocols.

## 7. Zero Tolerance
ICAN does not tolerate:
- Harassment  
- Discrimination  
- Violence or threats  
- Drug or alcohol use while volunteering  

Violation of this policy may result in removal.
