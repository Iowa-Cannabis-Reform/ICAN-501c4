# Victory Gardens – Volunteer Roles

## Garden Volunteer
Helps local gardens, events, plantings, and donation drives.

## Outreach Volunteer
Engages communities, shares resources, helps recruit new gardens.

## Education Volunteer
Teaches soil health, composting, herbalism basics, regenerative gardening.

## Event Volunteer
Manages registration tables, garden tours, community events.

## Digital Volunteer
Creates graphics, helps with social media, and email engagement.

All volunteers must follow ICAN’s volunteer handbook and code of conduct.
