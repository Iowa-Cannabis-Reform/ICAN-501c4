# Victory Gardens – FAQ

### Do I need a big garden?
No. Containers, raised beds, balconies, and indoor grow racks all count.

### What can I grow?
Any food crops, herbs, or non-cannabis botanicals.  
Cannabis growth is subject to state law.

### Do I need to donate food?
No—donation is encouraged but optional.

### How are gardens displayed publicly?
Only Sprout/Canopy/mobile partners show up on the public map, using city-level location.

### Can I be removed?
Only for safety, legal, or compliance violations.
