## Step 1: Form Submission
Participants submit the online registration form:
- Name
- Garden type
- City or region
- Contact email
- Tier preference
- Public listing opt-in

---

## Step 2: Internal Review
ICAN staff verifies:
- Completeness  
- Appropriate classification  
- Compliance with guidelines  

---

## Step 3: Approval & Tier Assignment
Each garden is assigned:
- Seed (default)  
- Sprout (approved)  
- Canopy (sponsored/paid)  

---

## Step 4: Welcome Packet
Automated email includes:
- Registration confirmation  
- Program overview  
- Tier benefits  
- Private or public listing status  
- Resource links  

---

## Step 5: Map Placement (if public)
Staff enters:
- City-level location  
- Tier  
- Optional garden photo  
- Contact form link  

---

## Step 6: Ongoing Engagement
- Monthly newsletters  
- Seasonal garden tips  
- Volunteer opportunities  
- Public events  

---

## Step 7: Annual Renewal
Gardens confirm:
- Tier selection  
- Participation status
