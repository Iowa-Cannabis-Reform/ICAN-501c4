# Victory Gardens – Host Garden Agreement

Thank you for participating in the Victory Gardens Program. This agreement outlines the expectations for gardens appearing publicly or hosting events.

## Expectations
Host gardens agree to:
- Uphold ICAN’s community guidelines  
- Maintain a safe, clean gardening space  
- Use regenerative or soil-conscious practices when possible  
- Provide accurate educational information  
- Follow local laws regarding produce donation  
- Remain nonpartisan during ICAN events  

## ICAN Provides
- Program materials  
- Resource library  
- Event collaboration  
- Public visibility via map & profile  
- Volunteer support (if requested)  

## Liability
- Hosts are responsible for safety on their property.  
- Host participation is voluntary and revocable.  

## Sign-Off
By participating in the program, hosts acknowledge acceptance of this agreement.
