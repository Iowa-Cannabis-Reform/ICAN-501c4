## Health & Safety
- Keep garden walkways clear  
- Provide handwashing or sanitizing options during events  
- Label plants clearly  
- Ensure tools are stored safely  
- Report hazards to ICAN for guidance  

---

## Produce Donation Rules (Iowa)
- Donated produce must be clean and edible  
- Avoid pesticide contamination  
- Harvest before over-ripeness  
- Follow local food bank instructions  

---

## Compliance Boundaries
To maintain C4 compliance:
- No candidate endorsements  
- No partisan messaging at garden events  
- Issue education is allowed  
- Voter education (nonpartisan) is allowed  

---

## Photo Permissions
- Obtain consent before photographing visitors  
- ICAN may feature gardens with permission
