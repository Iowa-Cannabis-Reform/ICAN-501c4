# Iowa Victory Gardens Map

Explore the growing network of home gardens, community gardens, farms, and partner sites taking part in the Victory Gardens Program.

This interactive map highlights Iowans who are:
- Growing food  
- Donating surplus produce  
- Teaching regenerative practices  
- Hosting community events  

Together, they form a statewide community rooted in generosity and resilience.
