# Victory Gardens Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

## Purpose
To outline how ICAN operates, supports, and manages the Victory Gardens Program and ensures C4 compliance.

## Sections
1. Program governance  
2. Registration and approval process  
3. Map listing standards  
4. Education and messaging rules  
5. Event hosting requirements  
6. Donation tracking  
7. Safety practices  
8. Volunteer involvement  
9. Data handling  
10. Annual reporting  

## Compliance
Victory Gardens must remain:
- Nonpartisan  
- Education-first  
- Community-centered  
- Compliant with ICAN's 501(c)(4) status
